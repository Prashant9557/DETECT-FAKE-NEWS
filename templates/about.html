Our Mission

At Fake News Detector, we believe the internet should be a space for truth and transparency. Our mission is to empower users to detect and combat misinformation using innovative AI tools and community collaboration.

We strive to create a more informed online world where everyone can make decisions based on accurate and verified content.

Our journey started with a simple idea: to provide everyone with tools that ensure accountability in digital content. Today, we continue to expand this mission worldwide.
